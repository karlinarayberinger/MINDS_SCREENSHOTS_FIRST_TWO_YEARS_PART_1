MINDS_SCREENSHOTS_FIRST_TWO_YEARS_PART_1

The images below capture some of the highlights of the past two years of the author’s development (in the form of Minds dot Com screenshots of the Minds dot Com channel belonging to @karbytes). The author joined the Minds social network in April 2020.

Each one of the images which is displayed on this page (in addition to that image’s respective GitHub repository page) was backed up to Archive dot Org using the WayBack Machine.

This repository contains multiple images and one HTML file which are associated with the marketing of Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com and the associated moniker karbytes between the years 2020 and 2021.
